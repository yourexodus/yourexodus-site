About Us

Your Exodus is more than a testimony website. It is a faith-centered community where people can preserve their testimony, discover hope through the experiences of others, grow through Bible study, document personal prayers and journals, and strengthen their relationship with God. We are the Francis family. This platform exists to give people a place to share, heal, and document life experiences that matter.

Our Mission

To create a safe place where people can preserve their testimony, encourage one another through authentic stories, and grow in Christ through prayer, Scripture, and community.
