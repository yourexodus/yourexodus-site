Who We Are

Sharing the hope of Jesus Christ, one testimony at a time.

Our Mission

Your Exodus is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to proclaiming the hope of the Gospel and reminding the world that every person has the opportunity to experience new life through Jesus Christ.

We are a body of believers united by one purpose: to share the Good News that death is not the end, but the beginning of eternal life for all who place their faith in Jesus Christ. In a world filled with fear, brokenness, disappointment, and uncertainty, we believe God's story of redemption is still being written in the lives of ordinary people.

The name Your Exodus represents more than a journey—it represents God's power to lead His people out of darkness and into His marvelous light. Just as God delivered Israel from bondage, Christ delivers us from sin, shame, fear, addiction, regret, and hopelessness. Every believer has an Exodus story, and every testimony points to the faithfulness of God.

As followers and disciples of Jesus Christ, we believe we have been commissioned to go and make disciples of all nations. Our mission is to encourage, equip, and inspire others by sharing biblical truth, personal testimonies, practical wisdom, and stories of God's transforming grace.

Whether you are searching for answers, rebuilding after loss, overcoming hardship, or simply seeking a deeper relationship with God, we want you to know there is hope. Your past does not define your future. God's grace is greater than your failures, and His love is stronger than your fears.

We believe every life has purpose, every story has value, and every person can become a testimony of God's redeeming power.

Our prayer is that through the content shared here, you will come to know Jesus Christ more deeply, grow in your faith, and discover the freedom found in walking with Him.

"The greatest Exodus is not simply leaving something behind—it is following Christ into the life He has prepared for you."
