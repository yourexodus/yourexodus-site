Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Your Exodus collects, uses, protects, and manages your information when you use our website and services.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Your Exodus, we recognize that the stories, prayers, journals, and testimonies shared within our community are deeply personal. We are committed to treating your information with care, integrity, and respect.

Our goal is to provide a secure, faith-centered environment where individuals can grow spiritually, encourage one another, and preserve meaningful moments without unnecessary collection or misuse of personal information.

By using the Your Exodus website, creating an account, or submitting content, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the platform, we may collect:

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted form)
  • Prayer requests
  • Journal entries
  • Testimonies
  • Bible study notes
  • Profile preferences
  • Technical information such as browser type, device type, and IP address for security purposes

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to operate, improve, and protect the Your Exodus platform. Specifically, we may use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Allow you to securely access your Prayer Center, Journal, Bible Study, and other dashboard features.
  • Display content that you choose to make public.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your questions.
  • Improve website functionality and user experience.
  • Protect the platform from fraud, spam, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Comply with applicable laws and legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Sharing Your Information

Your Exodus does not sell or rent your personal information.

Information may only be shared when necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable law or a lawful court order.
  • Protect the safety of our users or the public.
  • Investigate fraud, abuse, spam, or illegal activity.
  • Protect the rights and security of Your Exodus.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

These safeguards may include encrypted passwords, secure connections, authenticated user access, and routine monitoring for suspicious activity.

Although we strive to protect your information, no internet transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Privacy Choices

Depending on your use of the platform, you may have the ability to:

  • Update your profile information.
  • Change your password.
  • Delete your own journal entries or prayer requests.
  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Request removal of public content that you have submitted.

Certain information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate security and recordkeeping purposes.

Cookies and Analytics

Your Exodus may use cookies or similar technologies to improve website performance, remember user preferences, and understand how visitors use the site.

Cookies do not give us access to your computer or personal files. You may disable cookies within your browser settings, although doing so may affect certain website features.

Children's Privacy

Your Exodus is intended for individuals who are at least 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate parental consent where required by law.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information without permission, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or security practices.

The "Last Updated" date below will indicate when revisions were made.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through the Your Exodus Contact page.

We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a safe, respectful, and faith-centered community.
